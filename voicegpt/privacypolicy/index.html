Privacy Policy for Voice GPT

Effective Date: 20th Aug, 2023

This Privacy Policy outlines how KannansLab ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and safeguards information obtained from users ("you" or "your") of the Voice GPT Android application ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

  1. Voice Data: The App uses the OpenAI API to access and convert user voice recordings into text for further processing. We collect and store these voice recordings temporarily in order to facilitate the conversion process.
  2. Converted Text: The text resulting from the voice conversion process is also stored temporarily to enable the App to read it aloud to the user.
  3. Device Information: We may collect certain information about your device, including device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers. This information is used for technical purposes to improve the performance and compatibility of the App.

Use of Information

  1. Voice Conversion: The primary purpose of collecting and processing your voice recordings is to convert them into text using the OpenAI API. This text is then used for the subsequent reading functionality of the App.
  2. App Functionality: The converted text is used to enable the App to read aloud the content you've provided through voice input. This functionality is designed to enhance user experience and accessibility.
  3. Improvement of Services: We may use the information collected to analyze usage patterns, identify areas for improvement, and enhance the overall functionality and performance of the App.

Data Retention: We retain your voice recordings and converted text for a limited period of time necessary for the conversion process and the subsequent reading functionality. This data is stored securely and is not used for any other purpose.

Data Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services: The App uses the OpenAI API to facilitate the voice-to-text conversion process. Your use of the OpenAI API is subject to OpenAI's terms and privacy practices. We recommend reviewing OpenAI's Privacy Policy for further information.

Your Choices: You have the right to choose whether to provide voice recordings to the App. If you do not provide voice recordings, certain features of the App may not function as intended.
